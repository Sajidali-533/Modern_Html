What are enteties?
- The HTML enteties are strings of text that start with an ampersand symbol(&) and end with a semicolon(;). These enteties are commonly used to demonstrate characters that are reserved and imperceptible charactes such as non-breaking spaces.

List of 5 commonly used :
1. &amp; for (&)
2. &gt; for (>)
3. &lt; for (<)
4. &quot; for (")
5. &copy; for (copyright)
